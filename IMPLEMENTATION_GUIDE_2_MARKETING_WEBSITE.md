## PART A: CHOOSE YOUR APPROACH

### Option 1: Webflow (Recommended for Speed) ⭐⭐⭐

**What is Webflow?**
- Visual website builder (like Wix/Squarespace but for professionals)
- Hosted on Webflow's servers
- Drag-and-drop builder
- Professional output
- Great for marketing sites

**Pros:**
- ✅ Fast to build (2-3 days)
- ✅ Professional output
- ✅ SEO-friendly built-in
- ✅ Good templates available
- ✅ Easy to update
- ✅ Responsive (mobile-friendly)

**Cons:**
- ❌ Monthly cost (£12-36/month)
- ❌ Vendor lock-in
- ❌ Limited customization without code

**Cost:** £12/month (hosting + builder)  
**Time to launch:** 3-5 days  
**Recommendation:** **Use this** ⭐

**Steps:**
1. Go to: https://webflow.com
2. Sign up for free account
3. Browse templates (search "SaaS" or "Software")
4. Pick template you like
5. Customize: Your copy, colors, images
6. Connect domain (accountshub.co.uk)
7. Publish to live

**Template recommendations:**
- "Startup" or "SaaS" template
- Look for: Pricing page, Testimonials section, CTA buttons
- Should have: Hero, Features, Pricing, FAQ, Contact, Footer

## Step 6: Domain Connection (30 mins)

**Connect your domain to Webflow:**

1. **In Webflow:** Settings → Domains
2. **Click:** "Add domain"
3. **Enter:** accountshub.co.uk
4. **Choose:** "I already own this domain"
5. **Follow instructions** to update DNS records
6. **Whitelist domain** at your registrar (Namecheap/GoDaddy)
7. **Wait 24-48 hours** for DNS to propagate

**Then:** accountshub.co.uk → Your Webflow site ✓
