## STEP 1: Register at Companies House (UK)

### Online Registration (5 minutes)

**Go to:** https://www.gov.uk/register-a-company-online

**Information you'll need:**
1. **Company name:** AccountsHub Ltd (or your chosen name)
2. **Registered office address:** Your home address or business address
   - Example: 123 Main Street, London, SW1A 1AA
3. **Directors:** Your full name and address
4. **Shareholders:** Usually you (100%)
5. **Objects:** What the company will do
   - Suggested: "The development and provision of accounting and financial management software"

**Form to fill:** Form IN01 (included in online process)

**Steps:**
1. Go to Companies House website
2. Click "Register a company online"
3. If you don't have a WebFiling account, create one
4. Complete the form (takes 10 minutes)
5. Pay £12 (online)
6. Submit

**After submission:**
- You get a confirmation email within minutes
- Registration processed in 1-3 days
- You'll receive your **Company Number** (used for everything)
- Certificate of Incorporation arrives by post

## STEP 4: Draft Legal Documents

### Must-Have Documents

#### 1. **Terms of Service**
- Legal agreement between you and users
- Protects you from liability
- Must include appropriate clauses

**Don't write from scratch! Use templates:**
- **Termly.io** - Auto-generates T&Cs (£49/year)
- **Iubenda** - Easy website generator (€10/month)
- **OpenDoor** - Free basic version

**Key sections to include:**
- User responsibilities
- Payment terms (if paid tier)
- Liability limits
- Intellectual property
- Termination clause
- Dispute resolution

**Estimated cost:** £50-100 (one-time)

#### 2. **Privacy Policy**
- GDPR compliant (required for UK users)
- Explains data collection
- Required by law

**Use:** Same services as above (Termly, Iubenda)

**Key sections:**
- What personal data you collect
- How you use it
- Who you share it with
- User rights (GDPR)
- Contact information

#### 3. **Acceptable Use Policy** (Optional but recommended)
- What users can't do
- Prohibited content/abuse
- Security expectations

## STEP 5: Trademark Protection (Optional)

### Should You Trademark "AccountsHub"?

**Pros:**
- ✅ Protects your brand legally
- ✅ Prevents others using the name
- ✅ Looks professional
- ✅ Worth doing early

**Cons:**
- ❌ Costs £200-300
- ❌ Takes 3-6 months to process

**Recommendation:** **Do it after launch (month 3-6)**

**How to trademark:**
1. Go to: https://www.gov.uk/intellectual-property-an-overview/trade-marks
2. Register with **UKIPO** (UK Intellectual Property Office)
3. Cost: £170 per class
4. You'll use **Class 42** (software services)
5. Takes 4-6 months

**Simpler alternative:** Use **WorldMark** or **TrademarkNow** (handles filing for you)

### Logo Design

#### Option 1: Hire Designer (Recommended)
**Budget:** £300-500  
**Speed:** 1-2 weeks  
**Where:** Fiverr, 99designs, Upwork

**Brief to give designer:**
```
Company: AccountsHub
Tagline: "Professional accounts filing for small businesses"
Style: Modern, professional, tech-forward
Colors: Navy (#0b1829), Gold (#c9a84c), Cream (#f2ede3)
Usage: Website, app, social media, printed materials

Must be:
- Simple and memorable
- Works in black & white (for fax, etc.)
- Works at small sizes (favicon)
- Modern, not dated
- Unique (not generic)

- 3D effects
- Overly complex
- Trendy styles that date quickly
```

**Fiverr recommendations:**

### Brand Guidelines Document

Once you have a logo, create a **Brand Book** (2-3 pages):

**What to include:**

1. **Logo**
   - Full logo (use this most)
   - Logo symbol only (for favicon)
   - Do's and don'ts
   - Minimum size specifications

2. **Color Palette**
   ```
   Primary: Navy Blue #0b1829
   Secondary: Gold #c9a84c
   Tertiary: Cream #f2ede3
   Neutrals: Gray scale
   Accents: Greens (success), Reds (errors)
   ```

3. **Typography (Fonts)**
   ```
   Display Font: Playfair Display (headings)
   Body Font: IBM Plex Sans (body text)
   Code Font: Monaco or Courier (if showing code)
   ```

4. **Tone of Voice**
   - Professional yet approachable
   - Clear and concise
   - Helpful, not arrogant
   - Example: "AccountsHub makes accounts filing simple"

5. **Visual Style**
   - Examples of good usage
   - Examples of bad usage
   - Icon style
   - Photography style

**Where to create:** Figma (free), Canva, Adobe InDesign
