├─ Unlimited companies

├─ White-label option
├─ Single sign-on (SSO)
├─ Custom integrations
├─ Dedicated account manager
├─ Priority phone support
└─ SLA guarantee

- ✅ Users can try free (no risk)
- ✅ Free tier sustainable at scale
- ✅ Clear upgrade path
- ✅ Targets small businesses → medium businesses → enterprise

**Expected Conversion Rates:**
- Free users: 100s-1000s

- Enterprise for accountancy firms
